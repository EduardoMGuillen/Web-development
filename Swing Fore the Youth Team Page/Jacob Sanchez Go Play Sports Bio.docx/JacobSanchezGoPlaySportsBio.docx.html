Why I’m Passionate About Supporting Go Play Sports

Growing up, sports weren’t just about the game—they were about community, discipline, confidence, and opportunity. That’s why supporting Go Play Sports means so much to me.

Too often, families with limited income have to say “no” to youth sports simply because of the cost. Go Play Sports steps in to change that. By providing funds through grants and scholarships, we help cover registration fees so that no child is priced out of the chance to play.
