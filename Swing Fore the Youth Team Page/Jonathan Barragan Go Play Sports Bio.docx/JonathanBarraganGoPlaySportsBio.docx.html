Why I’m passionate about helping Go Play Sports

Some of the most important lessons in life—teamwork, discipline, resilience—aren’t learned in a classroom. They're learned on the field, on the court, and in the game. That's why I'm so passionate about supporting Go Play Sports.

Every child deserves the chance to play, grow, and feel like they belong. But for many families, the cost of youth sports can be a real barrier. Go Play Sports helps break that barrier by offering scholarships and grant support that allow kids from all backgrounds to participate.
