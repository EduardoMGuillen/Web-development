Mike Dannelly Bio

Short Bio for Go Play Sports Website

Mike Dannelly is a San Antonio real estate agent with eXp Realty, known for blending nearly 30 years of sales and leadership experience with a personal, fun approach to helping clients buy and sell homes. Beyond real estate, he founded Lone Star Improv, teaches youth and adults the power of “yes, and” through improv comedy, and performs stand-up comedy across Texas.

As a board member of Go Play Sports, Mike is passionate about ensuring all kids have the chance to experience the teamwork, discipline, and joy of organized sports. His guiding principle—“Inspiration over Obligation”—shapes his mission to inspire, connect, and uplift others both on and off the field.
