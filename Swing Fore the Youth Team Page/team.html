Our Leadership Team

Meet the dedicated individuals who make Swing Fore The Youth possible. Each team member brings unique expertise and unwavering commitment to our mission of supporting San Antonio youth through sports.

Roger Guerrero

Founder & Director

Roger Guerrero is the passionate force behind NZone Sports CenTex and Go Play Sports Inc., a youth sports organization that's redefining community engagement in San Antonio.

His journey began in the 1970s when his parents opened a local sports complex, planting the seeds of a lifelong commitment to youth development through play. During COVID-19, Roger saw children losing access to physical activity and launched programs serving over 2,000 children annually.

Roger's mission is to level the playing field by removing financial barriers, giving every child the chance to "Unplug and Have Fun" and "Get Out and Play."

Su Jin Letellier

Vice Chair

Su Jin Letellier is an Employer-Sponsored Benefits Advocate with DEBS Insurance, helping businesses transform employee benefits into strategic, tax-advantaged tools for growth.

As Vice Chair of Go Play Sports Inc., Su brings her passion for equity and empowerment to support underserved youth in San Antonio. A former athlete herself, she believes the values learned on the field—resilience, teamwork, leadership—drive meaningful change in business and life.

For Su, it's all about giving people the tools to thrive—whether that's in the workplace or on the field.

Vilma Tovar

Founder & Owner - CreativeIQ Marketing

Vilma Tovar is the Founder and Owner of CreativeIQ Digital Marketing, a Texas-based digital agency helping businesses grow through innovative strategies and measurable results.

Her community impact includes supporting initiatives like Swing for the Youth and the Two Step Gala with Team Ability. She speaks at universities and provides mentorship by taking in students to give them a head start in the professional world.

Through her leadership, Vilma bridges business growth with community impact—helping organizations tell their stories, expand their reach, and create lasting change.

Mike Dannelly

Board Member & Real Estate Agent

Mike Dannelly is a San Antonio real estate agent with eXp Realty, blending nearly 30 years of sales and leadership experience with a personal, fun approach to helping clients buy and sell homes.

Beyond real estate, he founded Lone Star Improv, teaching youth and adults the power of "yes, and" through improv comedy, and performs stand-up comedy across Texas.

As a board member of Go Play Sports, Mike is passionate about ensuring all kids have the chance to experience teamwork, discipline, and joy of organized sports. His guiding principle—"Inspiration over Obligation"—shapes his mission to inspire, connect, and uplift others.

Paul McGehee

Golf Tournament Committee Member

Paul McGehee serves on the golf tournament committee for Go Play Sports Inc. His family strongly believes in giving back to the community, and supporting kids is especially meaningful to them.

Paul runs 360 Painting of NW San Antonio & 360 Painting of Castle Hills, where they serve local families and businesses. He sees giving back through this tournament as another way to invest in the community they're blessed to work in every day.

Paul believes that children don't get to choose their circumstances, so when we have the chance to help open doors for them, it matters.

Jacob Sanchez

Licensed Agent - New York Life

Jacob Sanchez is a Licensed Agent with New York Life Insurance Company and a Registered Representative offering securities products through NYLIFE Securities LLC.

He works closely with business owners to implement tax-saving strategies, build retirement solutions, and create succession plans. He also supports families by providing guidance on life insurance, college funding, and investment strategies.

Growing up, sports weren't just about the game—they were about community, discipline, confidence, and opportunity. That's why supporting Go Play Sports means so much to him, as it helps cover registration fees so no child is priced out of the chance to play.

Jonathan Barragan

Licensed Agent - New York Life

Jonathan Barragan is a Licensed Agent with New York Life Insurance Company and a Registered Representative offering securities through NYLIFE Securities LLC.

He specializes in guiding nonprofit organizations through their financial journey, helping them implement tax-efficient strategies, build tailored retirement solutions, and develop succession plans that support their long-term mission and sustainability.

Jonathan believes that the most important lessons in life—teamwork, discipline, resilience—aren't learned in a classroom but on the field, court, and in the game. That's why he's passionate about supporting Go Play Sports.

Fabian

Founder - To Be Developed Project Services

Born and raised in San Antonio, Fabian founded To Be Developed – Project Services LLC to channel his passion for construction and his love for his hometown.

His firm has evolved into a respected construction and development company specializing in residential construction, restoration, and real estate projects with a focus on respecting each client's budget and vision.

Fabian's collaborative mindset extends beyond his business: he builds relationships across the real estate and construction community, mentors emerging contractors, and engages in initiatives that strengthen San Antonio's neighborhoods.

Our Mission

We believe every child deserves the opportunity to participate in sports, regardless of their family's financial situation. Through scholarships, grants, and community support, we're opening doors for San Antonio youth to experience the life-changing power of team sports.
