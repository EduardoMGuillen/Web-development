Vilma Tovar – Founder & Owner of CreativeIQ Marketing

Vilma Tovar is the Founder and Owner of CreativeIQ Digital Marketing, a Texas-based digital agency that helps businesses grow through innovative digital strategies and measurable results. CreativeIQ offers website development, SEO, social media management, Google Ads, CRM & automation systems, and professional video content—serving clients across healthcare groups, law firms, and nonprofit organizations.

Vilma’s community impact includes supporting initiatives like Swing for the Youth and the Two Step Gala with Team Ability, as well as giving back through education. She has spoken on panels and workshops at Texas A&M, Palo Alto College, Hallmark University, and Harmony High Schools, inspiring students to pursue leadership, creativity, and entrepreneurship. She also provides mentorship by taking in students to give them a head start in the professional world.

Through her leadership, Vilma bridges business growth with community impact—helping organizations tell their stories, expand their reach, and create lasting change.
