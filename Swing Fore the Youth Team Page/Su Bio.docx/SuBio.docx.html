Bio

Short version

Su Jin Letellier is an Employer-Sponsored Benefits Advocate, licensed agent, and plan designer with DEBS Insurance, a firm that has been fighting healthcare carrier greed since 1969. Su helps employers transform their benefit plans from costly obligations into strategic, tax-advantaged tools for growth—always rooted in fairness, empowerment, and doing what’s right.

That same passion fuels her role as Vice Chair of Go Play Sports Inc., a nonprofit dedicated to giving underserved youth in San Antonio access to the life-changing power of team sports. A former athlete herself, Su believes the values learned on the field—resilience, teamwork, leadership—are the same values that drive meaningful change in business and in life. For Su, it’s all about giving people the tools to thrive—whether that’s in the workplace or on the field.

Long Version

Su Jin Letellier is an Employer-Sponsored Benefits Advocate, licensed agent, and plan designer with DEBS Insurance, a firm fighting healthcare carrier greed since 1969. Su helps businesses turn employee benefits from costly obligations into strategic, tax-advantaged tools for growth—always with a focus on fairness and impact.

Her commitment to equity and empowerment extends into the community through her role as Vice Chair of the Go Play Sports Inc. committee, supporting underserved youth in San Antonio. The values behind DEBS—fighting for the underdog, doing what’s right—align perfectly with Go Play’s mission to get kids off screens and into team sports, where they build confidence, teamwork, and resilience.

As a former athlete and lifelong believer in the transformative power of sports, Su knows that the lessons learned on the field—teamwork, perseverance, leadership, and critical thinking—mirror the very principles that guide her work at DEBS. For Su, it’s all about giving people the tools to thrive—whether that’s in the workplace or on the field.
