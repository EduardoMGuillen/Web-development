Our Probate Process

A step-by-step guide to probate administration

1

Initial Consultation

We meet with you to understand your situation, review available documents, and explain the probate process and your options.

1-2 weeks
2

Probate Filing

We prepare and file the necessary probate documents with the court, including the application for probate and supporting documentation.

2-3 weeks
3

Asset Inventory

We help identify, locate, and inventory all estate assets, including real estate, personal property, and financial accounts.

1-2 months
4

Creditor Claims

We handle the legal notice process for creditors and manage the payment of valid debts and claims against the estate.

2-4 months
5

Tax Matters

We handle all tax obligations, including final income tax returns, estate tax returns, and any necessary tax planning strategies.

1-3 months
6

Asset Distribution

We facilitate the distribution of assets to beneficiaries according to the will or state law, ensuring proper documentation.

1-2 months
7

Final Settlement

We prepare final accounting and seek court approval for the distribution, officially closing the probate proceedings.

1-2 months
