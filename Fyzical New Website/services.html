Specialty Services

Advanced treatments and specialized services to complement your rehabilitation journey

Massage Therapy

Therapeutic massage to reduce pain, improve circulation, and promote relaxation and healing.

Anti-Gravity Treadmill

Advanced rehabilitation technology that reduces weight-bearing stress while maintaining cardiovascular benefits.

Dry Needling

Minimally invasive technique to release muscle tension and reduce pain through targeted needle therapy.

Thermal Therapy

Heat and cold therapy applications to reduce pain, inflammation, and promote healing.

Electrical Stimulation

Advanced electrical stimulation techniques to improve muscle function and reduce pain.

Ultrasound Therapy

Deep tissue therapy using sound waves to promote healing and reduce inflammation.
