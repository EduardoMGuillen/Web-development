Direct Access to Physical Therapy

In many states, you can now access physical therapy services directly without a physician referral. This means faster access to care and quicker recovery times.

Direct access allows you to see a physical therapist immediately for evaluation and treatment, reducing delays in your recovery process and potentially saving you time and money.

  • No physician referral required in most states
  • Faster access to care and treatment
  • Reduced healthcare costs
  • Immediate evaluation and treatment
  • Convenient scheduling options
  • Expert assessment and care planning
