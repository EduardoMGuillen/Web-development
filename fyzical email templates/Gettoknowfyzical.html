Welcome to Excellence in Physical Therapy

FYZICAL South San Antonio is a medical and scientific-based physical therapy and fitness organization with a broad range of expertise. You will find that we are much more than your typical physical therapy clinic.

Our goal at FYZICAL is to help you discover how to achieve the pinnacle of healthy living by expanding your physical boundaries and maximizing your health. We work to help you feel younger, more fit, healthier, and to love your life.
