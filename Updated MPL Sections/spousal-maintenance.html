The Spousal Maintenance Process

Understanding the steps involved in spousal maintenance proceedings in Texas.

1

Financial Assessment

We review both spouses' financial situations, including income, expenses, and ability to pay or need for support.

2

Support Calculation

We calculate appropriate spousal maintenance amounts using Texas guidelines and relevant factors.

3

Petition Filing

We file the appropriate spousal maintenance petition with the court and serve the other party.

4

Discovery Process

We gather financial documentation and evidence to support the spousal maintenance calculation.

5

Court Proceedings

We present your case in court, advocating for fair spousal maintenance arrangements.

6

Order Enforcement

We ensure spousal maintenance orders are properly enforced and take action when payments are not made.
