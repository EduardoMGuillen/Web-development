The Protective Orders Process

Understanding the steps involved in protective order proceedings in Texas.

1

Initial Consultation

We review your situation and determine whether you need to obtain or defend against a protective order.

2

Evidence Gathering

We gather evidence, documentation, and witness statements to support your protective order case.

3

Petition Preparation

We prepare the protective order petition or response, ensuring all legal requirements are met.

4

Filing & Service

We file the protective order petition with the court and serve the other party according to Texas law.

5

Court Hearing

We represent you in court, presenting evidence and arguments for or against the protective order.

6

Order Enforcement

We help enforce protective orders and take action when violations occur.
