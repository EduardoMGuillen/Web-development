The Uncontested Divorce Process

Understanding the steps involved in an uncontested divorce in Texas.

1

Initial Consultation

We review your situation and confirm that your divorce qualifies as uncontested under Texas law.

2

Agreement Review

We review all agreements regarding property division, child custody, and support arrangements.

3

Document Preparation

We prepare all necessary legal documents including the divorce petition and settlement agreement.

4

Filing & Service

We file the divorce petition with the court and serve the other party according to Texas law.

5

Court Hearing

A brief court hearing where the judge reviews and approves your uncontested divorce agreement.

6

Final Decree

The court issues the final divorce decree, officially ending your marriage.
