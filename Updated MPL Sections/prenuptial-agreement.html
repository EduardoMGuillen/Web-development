The Prenuptial Agreement Process

Understanding the steps involved in creating a comprehensive prenuptial agreement.

1

Initial Consultation

We discuss your assets, goals, and concerns to determine what should be included in your prenuptial agreement.

2

Asset Disclosure

We help you identify and document all assets, debts, and financial interests to be addressed in the agreement.

3

Agreement Drafting

We draft a comprehensive prenuptial agreement that addresses your specific needs and complies with Texas law.

4

Review & Negotiation

We review the agreement with both parties and facilitate negotiations to reach mutually acceptable terms.

5

Legal Review

We ensure both parties have independent legal representation and the agreement meets all legal requirements.

6

Execution

We oversee the proper execution of the prenuptial agreement before marriage to ensure legal validity.

What Prenuptial Agreements Can Address

Comprehensive prenuptial agreement representation for all types of marital and financial matters.

🏠

Property Division

Define how property will be divided in case of divorce, including pre-marital assets and marital property.

💰

Spousal Support

Address spousal maintenance, alimony, and financial support arrangements in case of divorce.

🏢

Business Interests

Protect business ownership, professional practices, and intellectual property from marital property claims.

💎

Inheritance Protection

Protect inheritance rights and ensure family assets remain separate from marital property.

📊

Debt Responsibility

Define responsibility for pre-marital debts and how marital debts will be handled.

Financial Planning

Address retirement accounts, investments, and other financial planning considerations.
