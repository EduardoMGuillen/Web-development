The Contested Divorce Process

Understanding the complex steps involved in contested divorce proceedings.

1

Initial Filing

We file the divorce petition and serve the other party, beginning the contested divorce process.

2

Discovery Phase

We gather evidence through depositions, document requests, and financial investigations.

3

Mediation Attempts

We attempt mediation to resolve disputes before proceeding to trial, when appropriate.

4

Pre-Trial Motions

We file motions for temporary orders, protective orders, and other pre-trial relief.

5

Trial Preparation

We prepare witnesses, organize evidence, and develop trial strategies for court proceedings.

6

Court Trial

We present your case in court, advocating aggressively for your rights and interests.
