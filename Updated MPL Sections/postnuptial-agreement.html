The Postnuptial Agreement Process

Understanding the steps involved in creating a comprehensive postnuptial agreement.

1

Initial Consultation

We discuss your assets, goals, and concerns to determine what should be included in your postnuptial agreement.

2

Asset Disclosure

We help you identify and document all assets, debts, and financial interests to be addressed in the agreement.

3

Agreement Drafting

We draft a comprehensive postnuptial agreement that addresses your specific needs and complies with Texas law.

4

Review & Negotiation

We review the agreement with both parties and facilitate negotiations to reach mutually acceptable terms.

5

Legal Review

We ensure both parties have independent legal representation and the agreement meets all legal requirements.

6

Execution

We oversee the proper execution of the postnuptial agreement to ensure legal validity and enforceability.

What Postnuptial Agreements Can Address

Comprehensive postnuptial agreement representation for all types of marital and financial matters.

🏠

Property Division

Spousal Support

Business Interests

Protect business ownership, professional practices, and intellectual property from marital property claims.

Inheritance Protection

Protect inheritance rights and ensure family assets remain separate from marital property.
