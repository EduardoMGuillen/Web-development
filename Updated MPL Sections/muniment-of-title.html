Muniment of Title

Simplified Probate Process for Property Transfer

What is Muniment of Title?

Muniment of Title is a simplified probate procedure in Texas that allows for the transfer of property when a valid will exists. This streamlined process is faster and less expensive than traditional probate administration, making it an attractive option for many families.
